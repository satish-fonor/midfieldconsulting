Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

This page explains what information Midfield Consulting may collect through the website, bookings, email, consultations, and related follow-up, and how that information may be used.

1. Information We May Collect

  • Contact information you send by email or through any contact form we make available, such as your name, email address, company, and message.
  • Booking information related to consultations, such as the event type, date and time, invitee name, and contact details.
  • Payment-related information handled through third-party providers such as Stripe. We do not expect to store full payment card details on this site.
  • Consultation preparation information you choose to share before a session, including project context, workflow details, system descriptions, or prototype notes.
  • Future intake responses if we add a post-booking intake flow.
  • Meeting-related information, including calendar details, Google Meet delivery details, and recordings if a meeting is recorded.
  • Basic technical information such as email metadata, browser or device details, and site usage logs if those are generated by our hosting or service providers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries.
  • To schedule, confirm, and deliver consultations.
  • To prepare for meetings and provide advisory services.
  • To follow up after a consultation.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the website and related service workflows.
  • To keep records needed for billing, support, dispute resolution, and legal compliance.

3. Third-Party Services

Booking, payment, communications, and meeting delivery may involve third-party providers. These may include Calendly for scheduling, Stripe for payment processing, and Google Meet or Google Workspace for meetings, calendar invitations, and related communications. Their own terms and privacy practices also apply to your use of those services.

4. Confidentiality and Sensitive Information

Information shared for consultations will be treated as confidential and used to prepare for and conduct the consultation. Please avoid sharing passwords, private keys, highly sensitive regulated personal data, or anything you are not authorized to disclose. You may anonymize names, customer data, and internal identifiers where appropriate.

5. Recordings

Consultations may be conducted through Google Meet and may be recorded for note-taking, quality, and follow-up purposes. By participating, you consent to recording where applicable. If something is especially sensitive, please say so and avoid sharing anything you do not want recorded.

6. Email and Follow-Up

We may send follow-up emails related to inquiries, bookings, consultations, payment confirmations, intake steps, and service delivery. If we send marketing emails in the future, they should include an appropriate way to opt out. Transactional or service-related messages may still be sent when needed to deliver a booked consultation or related service.

7. Data Sharing

We may share information with service providers only as reasonably needed to operate the site, schedule and deliver consultations, process payments, support communications, or comply with legal obligations. We do not use consultation or inquiry information as a product for sale.

8. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably needed to respond to inquiries, deliver consultations, maintain records, resolve disputes, and meet legal or operational obligations. Different categories of information may be kept for different periods.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and operational measures designed to protect the information we handle. No system can guarantee perfect security, and you should use judgment about what information you choose to share.

10. Your Choices

If you want to ask about information you have shared with us, or request correction or deletion where appropriate, contact us and we will review the request in light of the information we have and any legal or operational need to keep it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version here and revise the effective date.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@midfieldconsulting.com, consult@midfieldconsulting.com, or sales@midfieldconsulting.com.
