About

A small, founder-led practice built around real operating problems.

Midfield exists for teams that already explored what AI can do and now need direct help turning that early momentum into software that can keep up with real business use.

What Midfield is

  • Founder-led consulting
  • Focused on internal tools and workflows
  • Practical help, not high-level theater
  • Small enough to stay hands-on

Why Midfield exists

Many teams can get a first AI workflow working. Much fewer know how to turn that into dependable business software.

Fast experiments create new kinds of mess

AI tools made it possible for business builders to move quickly. That speed is useful, but it also creates fragile systems, unclear ownership, and technical debt much earlier than before.

That is where Midfield fits

The work is about helping you understand what you already built, identify what is worth keeping, and move toward a more stable version without overcomplicating it.

How we work

Small scope first, clear next steps, and direct technical judgment.

Start from the workflow

The point is not to make the software feel advanced. The point is to make the business process work better.

Use plain language

You should understand what is broken, what matters, and what the next decision actually is without translating buzzwords.

Keep the build proportionate

Not every prototype deserves a full rebuild. The right answer is often a narrower, cleaner, more practical version.

Founder / credibility

Satish Murthy

What that means in practice

You are working with someone focused on the software, the workflow, and the actual tradeoffs in front of you, not someone trying to expand scope before the fundamentals are clear.
