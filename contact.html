What happens next

The first step is usually to determine whether a 30 minute or 60 minute consultation is the right fit.

Good first messages usually include

  • What you built
  • What is currently breaking or slowing you down
  • Whether you want advice, cleanup, or a second opinion
  • Any specific question you want answered first

If you would rather start by email, that is completely fine. A short plain-language note is enough to begin.
